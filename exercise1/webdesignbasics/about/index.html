Get to know me

Hi there!

  1. Parsons School of Design where I majors in Strategic Design and Management and minors in Communication Design.
  2. Grew up in Boston but currently living in Bushwick!

I know for certain I'm driven by beauty. I find beauty in complex problems or products and experiences that appeal to the eye. My mission is to work with others around me who aren’t afraid of dreaming big to make a positive influence in the world. My responsibility is to be the one eager to learn and to use my knowledge in ways the public doesn’t normally see it.

I believe the future will be filled with more designers and art won’t be seen as an unworthy education. Everyone in a sense is a design thinker, it just depends on what they focus on. For me, I want to improve technology and lead the direction for change on our society.
